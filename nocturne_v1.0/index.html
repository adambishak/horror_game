About the game:

The goal of the game is to keep your character alive and kill enemies.
By killing enemies, the player gains points.
These points can then be used to unlock checkpoints and move on to further levels.
Throughout the game, the player will read through a story of the character and their state of depression.
Finish the game to gain insight into the character’s mental state and how their life continues. (Hint: it’s all an illusion!)
